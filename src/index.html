GAME SETUP

Claim Territory:

Claim wisely, choose stars that may lead you to gaining a unit bonus. Phase ends after all stars are claimed.

Initial Placement:

Each player gets a total of 9 units to place over the course of 3 rounds. Strengthen stars that could lead you to gaining a unit bonus.

GAME BEGINS

Placement Phase:

Place units that you receive from bonuses and territory count before beginning attack.

Battle Phase:

You may attack enemy stars adjacent to stars you control that have one or more units.
